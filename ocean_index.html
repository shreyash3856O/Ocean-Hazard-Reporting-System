Key Features

Our integrated platform combines citizen reporting with advanced analytics for comprehensive ocean hazard monitoring

Geotagged Reporting

Citizens can submit precise location-based reports with photos, videos, and descriptions of observed ocean hazards.

Social Media Analytics

Real-time analysis of public discussions on social media to detect hazard-related trends and sentiment.

Interactive Dashboard

Dynamic visualization of hazard reports and social media activity on an interactive map with real-time updates.

Multilingual Support

Platform accessible in multiple regional languages to ensure inclusivity for all coastal communities.

Offline Capability

Functionality to collect data offline and sync when connectivity is restored, essential for remote areas.

Role-based Access

Secure access controls for citizens, volunteers, officials, and analysts with appropriate permissions.
